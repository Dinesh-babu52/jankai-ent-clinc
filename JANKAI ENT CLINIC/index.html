Advanced Ultrasound Diagnostics

Advanced ultrasound diagnostics use high-frequency sound waves to create detailed images of internal organs and tissues. This non-invasive technique helps detect abnormalities, monitor conditions, and guide treatments accurately.

Rigid Scope

An ear endoscope is a slender, tube-shaped medical device equipped with a light and camera. It provides a clear, magnified view of the ear canal and eardrum, aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of various ear-related conditions and infections.

Digital otoscope

A digital otoscope is a modern, handheld medical device used to examine the ear canal and eardrum. It uses a light source and a camera to capture high-resolution images or videos of the ear, which can be displayed on a screen.

Flexible Endoscopy

Flexible Endoscopy is a diagnostic procedure that uses a thin, flexible tube with a camera and light to examine the nasal passages and sinus openings. It helps identify infections, polyps, tumors, or structural issues causing nasal problems.

What ENT services are available at Janaki ENT Clinic?

We provide diagnosis and treatment for a range of ENT conditions such as sinusitis, hearing loss, throat infections, voice disorders, and perform endoscopic and surgical procedures as needed.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our clinic directly. We also accept walk-ins, but prior booking is recommended to minimize wait time.

Is ENT endoscopy a painful procedure?

ENT endoscopy is generally painless and performed under local anesthesia. It may cause slight discomfort for a few seconds but helps in accurate diagnosis.

Do you provide hearing tests and hearing aids?

Yes, we conduct pure tone audiometry (hearing tests) and provide guidance on hearing aids based on the patient’s condition and requirement.

Where are you located, and what are the clinic hours?

We have branches in Karaikkudi and Trichy. You can check our clinic timings and contact details on the “Contact Us” section of our website.
